About Me

My name is Jensen Bouzi and I grew up in Queens, NY. I recently graduated from Amherst College with a B.A. in Asian Languages and Civilizations and a minor concentration in Mathematics. Although I've had a unique undergraduate experience, seemingly unrelated to computer programming, I've had a passion for technology ever since joining an after school engineering program in the 7th grade.

I have experience developing applications with Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, PostgreSQL, RSpec, Sinatra, MVC, among other technologies and methodologies. My meticulous nature drives me to procude modular, flexible code with a proven track record for learning and implementing new technologies quickly. If I am not coding I am most likely enthusiastically watching a soccer game or heavily involved in a music debate with other music connoisseurs.
