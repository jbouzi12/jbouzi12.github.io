About Me

My name is Jensen Bouzi and I was born in Brooklyn but raised in Queens, NY. I recently graduated from Amherst College with a B.A. in Asian Languages and Civilizations and a minor concentration in Mathematics. Although I've had a unique undergraduate experience, seemingly unrelated to computer programming, growing up I've always enjoyed the technical aspect of math and the sciences, yet I loved expressing myself creatively through various outlets such as poetry, music, and futbol; I've been able to combine both through my newfound coding passion. I am currently a Dev Bootcamp student, on the road to becoming a web developer. I am a futbol fanatic, music enthusiast, aspiring world traveler, bi-linguist (English and Japanese), and a man who aims to continuously rectify himself in order to bring quality to the world.
