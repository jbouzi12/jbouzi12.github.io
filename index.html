Jensen Bouzi

About

Web Developer

Jensen Bouzi is a Web Developer from Queens, New York with nack for solving complex problems and a passion for learning that sees him pick up new technologies swiftly. A recent graduate of Amherst College where he recieved a B.A. in Asian Languages and Civilizations, minored in Mathematics, and gained fluency in Japanese, Jensen's journey into tech was quiet unorthodox. Growing up, he attended the Hayden Engineering & Applied Sciences program at Columbia University where he studied chemistry, physics, biology, robotics, and computer science. However after persuing various academic passions and traveling the world during his undergraduate years, it was only in his final year that he rekindled his passion for technology.

Since graduating from Amherst College in the spring of 2014, Jensen has rigorously ventured to expand his skillset and depth of coding knowledge by leveraging online resources, books, and even completing the intensive web development program, Dev Bootcamp. He has experience developing applications with Ruby on Rails, Sinatra, JavaScript, Angular, PostgreSQL, Test Driven Development, among other technologies and methodologies. Jensen's meticulous nature drives him to produce modular, flexible code with a proven track record for learning and implementing new technologies quickly.

If Jensen is not reading about JavaScript or attempting to re-solve a problem in half the amount of code, he is most likely enthusiastically watching his favorite soccer team, Arsenal, or heavily involved in a music debate with other music connoisseurs.

Time Tracker

Time Tracker is an interactive proposal for Tallan Inc.'s employee time management portal. Employees can log in and submit/edit new timesheets for the project or project phase that they were assigned to. In addition, administrators can manage their internal system of employees, clients, and client projects that employees are assigned to.

Building with:JavaScript, Bootstrap, AngularJS, HTML5 LocalStorage

PinPoint

PinPoint is a Chrome Extension that allows you to take notes on Youtube videos in real time and keep track of your annotated videos. Each note automatically has a time associated with it, allowing the user to easily jump to key moments in a video, and is saved and readily accessible for future reference.

Built with: JavaScript, Google-Chrome API

GameOverflow

GameOverflow is a web application that revolves around gaming. Users can create a profile, post articles on any game, genre, or console, and comment as well as vote on other user's content. Along with the Ruby on Rails backend, GameOverflow was built using Test Driven Development via an RSpec test suite, an ActiveRecord database, and HTML and CSS for our web design.

Built with: Ruby, Rails, TDD, RSpec, PostgreSQL, CSS

MiddleGround

MiddleGround is an app that helps two friends find interesting places to meet at a place between them so neither has to travel longer than the other. This app is unique because we find the midpoint based on travel time, not distance.

Built with: Ruby, Google Maps API, Yelp API, Sinatra, jQuery, AJAX
